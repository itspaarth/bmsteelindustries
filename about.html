The Legacy

Built on strength. Refined by time.

Since 1964, BM Steel Industries has been quietly shaping the foundations of everyday spaces. What began as a commitment to quality hardware has grown into a legacy of trust spanning generations.

We specialize in essential architectural hardware — from hydraulic door closers and precision hinges to modern modular kitchen systems. Our products are engineered to perform seamlessly and last significantly longer than the industry standard. Every fitting reflects a simple philosophy: it should just work — flawlessly.

For over six decades, we’ve earned the confidence of architects, builders, and homeowners alike by delivering consistency, unparalleled durability, and uncompromising quality standards. We don't build our reputation through noise; we build it through reliability.

Fully registered and GST-compliant, we operate with complete transparency at every level of our supply chain. Because trust isn’t claimed — it’s built, one flawless transaction at a time.

We don’t just supply hardware. We support the spaces people live and work in — quietly, beautifully, every day. As we move forward, our focus remains unchanged: to refine, to improve, and to deliver products that stand the test of time — just like we have.
